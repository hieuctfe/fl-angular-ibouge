Mission Statement


One world, together

iBouge's mission is to keep travelers connected, safe, and in-the-know. In light of the COVID-19 crisis, we understand that you care not only about your well-being but also the health of others. Social responsibility in regards to travel has changed. Today, it means more than just being eco-friendly but also about promoting physical safety from COVID-19. We get it, and we're here to help you make the best decisions about being dynamic in today's global society.

iBouge is a company that pins local information on map-interfaces and makes it available and easy to find to the broader global community. Our aim is to make community-based information universally accessible so that travelers, globetrotters, jetsetters, expatriates, business leaders and the globally-minded can rely on secure, trusted information about where they're going and what they're doing.

We have designed tools for sharing information about people, conversations, events and soon businesses, available to all users on iBouge. We value the health, safety and well-being of travelers and want to make sure you have the resources you need to thrive -- no matter where you are or want to go.

You can count on iBouge to provide state-of-the-art tools using location intelligence to keep travelers connected to the cities and communities that matter to them most. iBouge is a company that understands the world is interconnected, globalized and becoming closer than ever before. We know that people's lives exist in more than one location and you're always on the move. 

You can count on iBouge to keep you locally connected, anywhere you go.
